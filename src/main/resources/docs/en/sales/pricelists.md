# Price lists and price types

Price lists are used to store and apply prices in sales orders.

## Price types

A price type defines how the price is calculated:

- fixed price;
- price with markup;
- other rules (depending on configuration).

## Price list

A price list typically includes:

- price type;
- validity period;
- list of items and prices.

## Using in an order

When you add an order line, the system may fill in the price from the price list based on:

- price type;
- customer/terms;
- order date.
