---
title: Units of measure
---

The **“Units of measure”** directory stores the list of units used in items and document lines (for example, pieces, kilograms, meters).

## Units of measure list

The list typically shows:

- **Name**;
- **ID** (can be generated automatically).

## Unit of measure card

Typical fields:

- **Name**;
- **ID**.

## Restrictions

If a unit of measure is already used in items, deletion may be forbidden. It is recommended not to delete such entries, but to keep the list consistent (for example, do not create duplicates).
