title: Taxes
---

In “Invoicing”, taxes are used to calculate line amounts and document totals.

## Directories

Most commonly used:

- taxes;
- tax groups;
- linking taxes to [items](../masterdata/items.md)/services (if configured).

## Usage in documents

A tax can be set:

- in [bill](bills.md)/[invoice](invoices.md) lines;
- automatically based on [item](../masterdata/items.md) settings or document type settings (see [Settings and directories](settings.md)).

## Restrictions

A tax used in calculations can be protected from deletion.
